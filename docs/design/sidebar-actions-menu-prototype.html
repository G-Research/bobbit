Sidebar Actions Menu

Hover a row, then click the right-most hamburger. Existing quick-action icons remain clickable; the menu reveals labels and secondary actions.

The open animation moves shared icons from the hover strip into the menu. Dismiss with Escape, outside click, item selection, or the hamburger.

Ready. Keyboard: focus a hamburger, press Enter/Space/ArrowDown; use Arrow keys inside the menu.
